Famous Places to Visit in Canada!

Here are some must-see places if you're visiting Canada! Click on the images to learn more!

Niagara Falls, located in Southern Ontario across the Canada-USA border is one of the most popular Canadian tourist sites!

The CN Tower, located in Toronto, Ontario, is Canada's tallest tower, and was once the tallest freestanding tower in the world. Located right next to the Rogers Centre, home of the Blue Jays!

Banff National Park, located in western Alberta, contains one of the most beautiful sceneries in the world, like Lake Louise and Moraine Lake, and scenic hiking trails like Sulphur Mountain!
